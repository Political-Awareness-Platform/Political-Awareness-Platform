# Contributing to Political Awareness Platform

Thank you for your interest in contributing! Here are the steps:

1. **Check Issues**: Look at our Issues to see if there's something you'd like to work on.

2. **Fork & Create a Branch**: Fork the repository and create a branch for your changes.

3. **Make Your Changes**: Make your changes and ensure they pass our tests.

4. **Create a Pull Request**: Switch back to your master branch, update it with the latest changes from the official repository, and then update your feature branch from your local copy of master. Push your changes and create a new pull request.

5. **Update Your Pull Request**: If asked to update your PR, please do so.

Thank you for your contributions!
